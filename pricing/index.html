Questions

Quick answers for teams evaluating HTMLess

Can I use HTMLess for free right now?

Yes. The free self-hosted core is the real starting point today, and the GitHub repo plus Docker setup are the canonical way to get running.

Are the hosted prices live?

Not yet. They are intentionally shown as roadmap direction and early-access positioning so the market story is clear before hosted HTMLess formally launches.

Why show pricing before hosted launch?

Because agencies, product teams, and non-technical buyers often want to understand where the product is going even if they start with self-hosting first.

Start free now, then follow the roadmap as HTMLess grows up.
