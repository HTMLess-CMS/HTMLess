Phase 9
May-Jul 2026

Delivery Performance and Realtime

Next Big Push

Make HTMLess feel much closer to Sanity on delivery speed and live updates without abandoning the PostgreSQL plus REST architecture.

FocusPerformance, cacheability, realtime
  • `published_documents` read model for faster CDA responses

  • SSE or WebSocket updates for admin and preview instead of polling
